Quick Guide

How to use this app for busy city workers

When in doubt, check what is open, close, and right for your mood.

01

Choose your area

Tap the area closest to you, such as Marunouchi, Yaesu, Hibiya, Kasumigaseki, or Akasaka.

02

Filter by mood

Pick cafe, Japanese, sweets, or search by restaurant name to narrow the list quickly.

03

Check crowd level

Green is easier to enter, orange may be busy, and red may need a wait.

04

Use the map

Numbered pins group nearby places. Tap a group or press plus to zoom in.

05

Open details

Use Details to open the store website or map and confirm hours and exact location.

Recommended flow

Before meeting someone, use Cafe plus likely available only. For lunch, zoom into the map and start with nearby green pins.
